About Our Company

Clarity Laboratories is a team of independent psychologists dedicated to bringing the expertise of our profession to mental health assessments conducted in a primary care setting. Our company allows primary care physicians in a busy practice setting to take advantage of the power of psychological testing to aid in the diagnosis and treatment of patients in their care.

Through secured electronic media, we are able to acquire data directly from the patient and designated others that know the patient well. This allows us to create a detailed description of the patient's emotional and behavioral functioning across informants and settings. Using normative data, sensitive to age and gender, we analyse the information and generate a psychological report that is accessible and useful to the primary care physician.

Our assessment team gathers the data, analyzes the results, and creates a practical, useful laboratory report. Combining our empirically-based assessment with the medical history, observations and interviews conducted in the primary care office, physicians can diagnose and treat mental health conditions with greater skill and confidence.

Who We Are

Clarity Laboratories provides an opportunity for licensed independent psychologists to provide a valued service from the convenience of their secured computer terminal. Drawing upon an extensive research base of normal development, psychopathology and quantitative assessment, we are able to analyze the obtained data and produce a concise laboratory report. Our goal is to provide primary care physicians information critical to the diagnosis and treatment of patients in their care.
